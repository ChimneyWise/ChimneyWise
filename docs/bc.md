## Executive Summary
ChimneyWise is a software solution designed to optimize the design, construction, and maintenance of chimneys. The business case for ChimneyWise outlines the potential benefits, costs, and risks associated with the development and implementation of the software. The primary goal of ChimneyWise is to improve efficiency and reduce costs for construction companies, architects, and engineers involved in chimney projects.

## Problem Statement
The current process of designing and constructing chimneys is often time-consuming and prone to errors. Traditional methods rely heavily on manual calculations and outdated software, leading to inefficiencies and increased costs. Additionally, there is a lack of standardized tools and processes, which further complicates project management and quality control.

## Proposed Solution
ChimneyWise aims to address these issues by providing a comprehensive software solution that integrates advanced design tools, real-time collaboration features, and automated calculations. The software will enable users to create accurate chimney designs, streamline project workflows, and enhance communication among stakeholders.

## Objectives
- Intuitive design interface for easy use by architects and engineers.
- Automated calculations to reduce errors and save time.
- Real-time collaboration features to enhance communication among stakeholders.
- Standardized tools and processes to improve project management and quality control.
- Easy modification of calculations to accommodate changes in project requirements.
- Easy update materials and design parameters to reflect the latest industry standards and innovations.
- Easy update of the software to incorporate new features and improvements based on user feedback and technological advancements.
- Easy update of materials prices to ensure accurate cost estimation and budgeting for chimney projects.
- The software must support multiple languages, including Danish, English, German, and French, for all user-facing features and documentation.

## Options and Alternatives (0-scenario, 1-scenario, 2-scenario)
- **0-scenario**: Continue with the current manual processes and outdated software, which may lead to ongoing inefficiencies and increased costs.
- **1-scenario**: Develop and implement ChimneyWise, which has the potential to significantly improve efficiency and reduce costs, but requires an initial investment in development and training.
- **2-scenario**: Explore alternative software solutions or custom development options, which may offer different features and benefits, but also come with their own set of risks and costs.

## Cost-Benefit Analysis
### Costs

- **Development Costs**: The initial development of ChimneyWise will require investment in software development, including architecture, system design, and integration with industrial processes and ERP systems.
- **Training Costs**: Users will need to be trained on how to use the new software effectively.
- **Maintenance Costs**: Ongoing maintenance and updates will be necessary to ensure the software remains up-to-date and functional.
- **Implementation Costs**: There may be costs associated with integrating ChimneyWise into existing workflows and systems.
- **Opportunity Costs**: The time and resources spent on developing and implementing ChimneyWise could potentially be used for other projects or initiatives.
- **Additional Cost Structure**: Includes software licenses, cloud hosting, hardware, training/certifications, accounting/legal, marketing, insurance, and time spent on sales/administration. (See docs/bmc.md)

### Benefits

- **Increased Efficiency**: ChimneyWise can significantly reduce the time required for design and construction processes, leading to faster project completion.
- **Cost Savings**: By minimizing errors and optimizing workflows, ChimneyWise can help reduce overall project costs.
- **Enhanced Communication**: Real-time collaboration features improve communication among stakeholders, leading to better decision-making and project outcomes.
- **Improved Project Management**: Standardized tools and processes facilitate better project management and quality control.
- **Value Propositions**: Custom-built software tailored to industrial workflows, direct developer communication, modern/scalable architecture, integration with machines/ERP/production/reporting, agile delivery, and technical ownership. (See docs/bmc.md)
- **Revenue Streams**: Potential revenue includes fixed-price projects, hourly consulting, SaaS subscriptions, maintenance contracts, training/workshops, system integration, and DevOps management. (See docs/bmc.md)
